Grounding & Expanding Retreat is a coming together of seekers in the beautiful nature of Nederland Colorado for an intentional 5 days of growth and nourishment. This is a small and intimate curated event designed to help you find your way inward as a vehicle to expand your consciousness and life force. On this retreat you will learn multiple tools and modalities to embody higher vibrations within the body and mind. The embodiment tools and practices you will learn on this retreat will become the vehicle for transformative and expansive experiences that will go on to inform and empower your daily life.

THEME OF RETREAT

Theme of

Retreat

The theme of Grounding & Expanding plays off the knowledge that one can only grow as tall as their roots reach down. Achieving a life filled with health, love, purpose, satisfaction, contentment, playfulness, and a sense of harmony needs to include the physical and the metaphysical. We will do somatic embodiment practices tied in together with spiritual and philosophical inquiry. Grounding & Expanding is a time to stop and explore what is possible all while taking in Nature’s beauty amongst a group of like minded seekers.

WHO IS THE

Retreat for?

This retreat is designed for individuals who are transitioning to a new level in their life—whether in work, relationships, or other areas—and are seeking a healthier, happier, and more balanced way of being.

WHAT IS LEELA

WHAT IS

Leela?

Leela is an ancient transformative practice from India, presented in the form of a large board game with dice. It is designed to help people find answers to their most profound questions and connect with their higher selves, allowing them to remember their true values and correct life path.

ABOUT US

Peter has dedicated his life to learning philosophies from the East and the West, as well as learning different physical and embodiment practices. Peter has a Bachelor’s and Master’s in Philosophy from San Francisco State University and works individually and in groups teaching yoga, fitness, health and wellness practices. He brings a background of experience and study in meditation, esoteric practices and metaphysical paradigms, as well as western anatomy and science paradigms. Peter hopes to bring joy, inspiration and peace to all he comes in contact with and aims to give you tools to do and be the same in the world. You can find him trekking in the mountains, traveling the world or teaching at his home in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Liana Moon
Breathwork and Mindfulness Teacher.
Leela guide.

Meet Liana Moon, a dedicated mother, artist, and a certified mindfulness meditation and breathwork teacher for both children and adults in the community of Topanga, California. Liana’s spiritual journey led her to live in Asia for over six years, immersing herself in the authentic traditions of Buddhism, Hinduism, Vedic astrology, chigong and energy healing. With a wealth of knowledge gained from her experiences, Liana brings over a decade of dedicated teaching to her community. As a guiding force, she has transformed countless lives through the profound practices of mindfulness and breathwork.

Liana’s mission transcends the classroom; she is committed to supporting individuals on their personal growth journey. Her goal is to empower them with invaluable tools that align their decision-making with their true potential.

Join Liana on a transformative journey of self-discovery, where the power of breath and mindfulness paves the way for a more conscious and fulfilling life.
